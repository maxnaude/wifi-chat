Failure to abide by these rules could result in your profile being disabled.

  1. Respect others.

    You might not always agree with others comments, but please respect their right to have an opinion.

    No pornography or sexually suggestive content.

    No abusive language or name-calling will be tolerated.

  2. Use WiFi Chat safely.

    Don't share any personal information about you or anyone else (passwords, identity numbers, banking details or contact details).

    Do not arrange to meet with strangers you meet on this platform.

    Beware of scams and report any suspicious behavior.

  3. Do not spam.

    Don't post the same comment repeatedly in multiple chats.

    Don't post advertisements or product promotions.

  4. Do not share illegal content.

    Don't share links to illegal downloading sites or pirated copies of items.

  5. Don't break the site.

    Don't break the site or do anything that interferes with normal use of the site.

WiFi Chat administrators, Project Isizwe and the City of Tshwane take no responsibility for the any consequence of broken rules, personal injury or the loss of privacy or personal items. WiFi Chat is public and administrators of reserve the right to disable a user account in the event of one or more of the above mentions rules are broken.
